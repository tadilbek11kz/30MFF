About the Film

"The Last Leaf" is a poignant 15-minute short film that explores themes of friendship, hope, and the transformative power of art. Set in a bustling city during autumn, it tells the story of two young artists and how a single leaf changes their lives forever.

Directed by emerging filmmaker Sarah Johnson, this film has already garnered critical acclaim at several international film festivals.
