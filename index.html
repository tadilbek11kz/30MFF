About the Video

"Procrastination" is a witty 90-second video that showcases the all-too-familiar dance of avoiding important tasks. Through a series of fast-forwarded scenes, we see the protagonist engaging in various activities that look productive but are, in reality, just clever ways to procrastinate.

Created by an anonymous procrastinator, this video holds a mirror to our daily struggles with time management and productivity.
